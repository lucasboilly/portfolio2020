About the project

Technology supporting solidarity


This project was made as part of Hack4Help, a two-weeks hackhaton hosted by Hétic school in december 2019. Within a team, I had to imagine and create a digital solution to help the homeless people.

We have decided to create an online plateform that allows homeless people to eat for free, depending on their tastes and their alimentary restrictions like allergies. The food would come from unsold stocks and donations from restaurants and supermarkets.
